Services for Seniors
  • Telepractice allows clients and therapists to connect over an online communication service, allowing clients to be able to stay at home if necessary or more convenient.
  • We use a third party service designed specifically for medical practices, so all video calls will be treated with the respect and privacy afforded by the law.
  • Most commonly, we have clients that meet with our therapists one on one, which allows therapists to focus completely on your needs.
  • One on one meetings usually occur once a week.
  • Group therapy is an invaluable service that allows our clients to feel a sense of community.
  • Group therapy allows our clients to work on their skills with other clients.
