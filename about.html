About Speech Easy
Welcome to Speech Easy! Speech Easy was founded in February 2020 by Jordan Doyle. Doyle was determined to open his own speech therapy clinic to help those of all ages be able to tackle life with the best language skills they can have. Inspired by his significant other, who engaged in speech therapy at a young age due to hearing loss and has achieved amazing success in life, Doyle is determined to give that to others. Working along a small staff of hard-working speech therapists, he has been able to recognize that dream every day. The staff at Speech Easy is dedicated to their clients by creating unique, personable treatment plans for each person. Doyle Speech Therapy staff also goes to various schools to provide screenings for children to catch hearing & speech difficulties early. We are dedicated to help you and your loved ones. Please explore our website to learn about us and contact us with any questions you may have.
